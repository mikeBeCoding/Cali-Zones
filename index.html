    Welcome to CaliZones

    Why Calzones and Empanadas you ask?

    Portability is key. We know how hard it can be to sit down and eat when you’re pressed for time. We feel you shouldn’t have to slow down due to hunger striking. Here at CaliZones, we specialize in food that will keep you on the go without sacrificing your time and settling for something of lesser quality.


    Quality food begins with quality ingredients.

    We believe fresher food produces the best nutrients, and we know there is no substitute for quality, so we source our produce locally. All of our vendors grow our ingredients GMO-free.
